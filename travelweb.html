THE CITY OF GODS

Varanasi or Banaras is one of the oldest cities in the world. It is situated on the bank of river Ganga spreading over 1535 sq.km area in the state of Uttar Pradesh. It is located between the confluences of river Ganga and Varuna and river Ganga and Assi rivulet.Varanasi, also known as Kashi and Banaras, is one of the oldest continuously inhabited city on earth.
varanasi has also been a great center of learning for ages. Varanasi is associated with promotion of spiritualism, mysticism, Sanskrit and yoga.Varanasi is among the most revered religious destinations in India.
